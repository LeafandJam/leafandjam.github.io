Switch Modchip (SX Core)

Adding CFW onto a patched Nintendo Switch

SX Core

If your Switch was released around 2018 or later, than there is a good chance your Switch is patched. If you have an unpatched Switch then it makes adding CFW a lot easier. It would simply require an RCM Jig and a computer to flash the payload. To check whether your Switch is patched or not, enter your Switch's barcode here.

For a long time if you had a patched Switch there was nothing you could do. Until the SX Core was released by Team Xecuter. This makes it possible to hack any Switch released as well Switch Lites with the SX Lite.

Installing the modchip

Eradicatinglove has a full guide on Installing the SX Core which I highly recommend you follow.

Installing the modchip requires the following tools/materials:

  • Tri-Wing screwdriver
  • Philips screwdriver
  • Adhesive remover
  • Soldering iron / solder wire
  • Thermal paste
  • Sponger
  • Sellotape
  • Pliers
  • Needle
